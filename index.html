About our Gym

We are a small gym focused on building a great fitness community in Southside. We are big on coaching, education and the right people. We study and teach the mechanics of good movement, and focus on consistency and intensity. Our programming is based on the foundational principles of CrossFit, and peppered with technique and mobility work so that every level of member can not only participate but grow.

-John Smith, Head Coach
